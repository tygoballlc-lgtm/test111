First Step

Free In-Home Consultation

We come to you. You don't lift a finger.

Every great renovation starts with a real conversation — not a form or a bot. One of our senior designers visits your home to understand your space, your goals, and your lifestyle. No pressure. No sales pitch. Just honest advice from someone who's done this hundreds of times.

🏠
In-home site assessmentWe walk every room, take measurements, and document the existing conditions — so our design is grounded in reality, not assumptions.
🎯
Goal & lifestyle conversationWe ask how you actually live — cooking habits, storage needs, how many people use each space — so the design serves your real life.
💬
Honest budget discussionWe'll tell you what's realistic for your budget — and we'll never oversell. If a simpler solution works better, we'll say so.

Final Step

Final Walkthrough

We're not done until you love every detail.

Before we close out any project, we walk through every inch of the renovation together. You review every surface, every detail, every fixture. If anything isn't exactly right, we fix it on the spot — or schedule it immediately. Your approval is the only thing that ends the job.

🔍
Room-by-room inspectionWe go through a detailed punch list together — every cabinet, every tile, every fixture — checking that everything meets our standards and yours.
🛠️
Immediate punch-list resolutionAny items that need attention are addressed before we leave. Minor items within 48 hours. We don't consider the project complete until your list is empty.
📜
5-year warranty & documentationYou receive a written 5-year workmanship warranty, plus all manufacturer documentation for appliances, materials, and fixtures we installed.

Living Through a Renovation

What to Expect

Honest answers to what it's actually like to live through a renovation with us.

🔇
Noise & Dust
Demo weeks are loud. We use dust barriers and HEPA air scrubbers to contain particles. All work zones are sealed from the rest of your home.
✓ Dust barriers on all work zones
✓ HEPA air scrubber on site
✓ Daily end-of-day clean-up
✓ No work before 8am or after 5pm
👥
The Crew
Our full-time employees — not subcontractors — work in your home. The same faces, every day. You'll get to know them, and they'll treat your home like their own.
✓ Full-time employees, not subs
✓ Background checked & insured
✓ Same crew from start to finish
✓ Respectful & professional always

Communication
You'll never wonder what's happening. Your project manager is reachable by text or call, and you receive a written update with photos every Friday afternoon.
